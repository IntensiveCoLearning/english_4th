## I. What Is RWA — Legal claim

RWA (Real World Assets) refers to the tokenization of off-chain assets onto blockchain rails. These assets include:

-   Sovereign bonds (e.g., U.S. Treasuries)
    
-   Credit instruments (private credit, structured products)
    
-   Real estate
    
-   Commodities
    
-   Funds
    
-   Equities (synthetic or direct representation)
    

> what is the character of these property? why they are , not others?

Examples:

-   Ondo Finance — tokenized Treasuries
    
-   MakerDAO — RWA-backed DAI exposure
    
-   BlackRock — BUIDL tokenized fund
    
-   Franklin Templeton — on-chain money market fund
    

**Core concept:**

RWA = Legal claim on real-world cash flow, represented as programmable on-chain tokens.

It is not “putting assets on-chain.”

It is **making financial rights interoperable with DeFi infrastructure.**

* * *

# II. What Problem RWA Intended to Solve

RWA emerged to solve three structural problems in crypto.

* * *

## 1️⃣ Crypto Has Yield Scarcity

After DeFi Summer, yields were primarily:

-   Token inflation
    
-   Ponzi-like reflexive incentives
    
-   Leverage-based carry
    

When rates rose globally (2022+), U.S. Treasuries yielded 4–5%, while crypto native yield collapsed.

RWA reintroduces:

> Real-world risk-free rate into on-chain economy.

That changes everything.

It anchors DeFi yield to global monetary conditions.

## 2️⃣ Stablecoins Need Real Backing

Stablecoins like USDC/USDT hold off-chain assets.

Protocols like MakerDAO began diversifying into RWAs because:

-   Pure crypto collateral is volatile
    
-   Overcollateralization is capital inefficient
    
-   Stability requires non-correlated income streams
    

RWA provides:

-   Predictable yield
    
-   Lower volatility
    
-   Balance sheet strength
    

It transforms DeFi from reflexive speculation → into credit-based system.

## 3️⃣ Traditional Finance Is Operationally Inefficient

TradFi problems:

-   T+2 settlement
    
-   Restricted access
    
-   High minimums
    
-   Geographical silos
    
-   Manual compliance processes
    

Tokenization offers:

-   24/7 settlement
    
-   Fractionalization
    
-   Global capital access
    
-   Programmable compliance
    

It compresses:

> Legal wrapper + asset servicing + transfer agent + settlement into smart contracts.
